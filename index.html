Anti-Cheat
Three layers
of proof.
01

AI Photo Analysis

Claude analyses every tournament entry photo. Must show the fish clearly on a measurement board with a visible ruler. AI reads the measurement and checks species. If uncertain → flagged for mod review.

02

GPS Waterway Check

When you enter a tournament, your GPS location is captured. We check you're within 10km of a recognised waterway — ocean, harbour, river or lake. No water nearby, no entry.

03

Mod Review Queue

AI-flagged entries go to human mods before hitting the leaderboard. Mods see the photo, AI reasoning and GPS data. Approve or reject with one click.
